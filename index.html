Bring Your A Game
A participation campaign that combined the netball players our audience love, with what they love doing on their phones. Making the Samsung Galaxy A5 the netball phone for 2017, while driving sales to Telstra.
In 2016
The highly successful Samsung #RethinkRoleModels campaign laid the foundations for Samsung’s netball partnership. Giving the sport and players the recognition they deserved.
In 2017
The challenge for Samsung and Telstra was to make the Samsung Galaxy A5 the netball phone for the Super League season, and drive sales to Telstra.
All we needed was a way to take our partnership to the next level.
The answer was on the social pages of netball fans and players alike. Trends that had everyone using their phones to film and post videos of themselves flipping bottles, pulling off amazing athletic feats, and well, just stand really-still.
We called this behaviour ‘bringing your A Game’. And the Samsung Galaxy A5 was the perfect phone to capture your A Game with.
Now all we needed to do was find a way to get it top of mind and into the hands of our audience.
Enter our Samsung Ambassadors and netball stars: Sharni Layton, Kim Ravallion, Paige Hadley and Caitlin Basset. Championing an integrated Bring Your A Game campaign across TV, social, digital broadcast, experiential at Super Netball Games and within the Telstra Netball Live app. Where they brought their A Game with the Samsung Galaxy A5 phone, and then challenged Australia to bring theirs. Kicking things off the Ambassadors posted short teaser videos on their social channels, with #GalaxyAGame.
Launch Film
The teasers were promptly followed by a fun, fast and full-on call-to-action video. In which the Netball Ambassadors captured and posted their A Game skills on a Samsung Galaxy A5. Which fans and social followers matched, with A Game skills of their own.
Netball association integration
Leveraging our partnership with the Netball association. The campaign continued with videos of players from 8 association teams in the league, capturing and bringing their A Game on social, digital and in broadcast. Where viewers voted to determine, which association brought the best A Game video for the chance to win a money can’t buy prize.
Super Netball activation
At Super Netball Games, the Bring Your A Game activation put the Samsung Galaxy A5 in the hands of netball fans. With an interactive app that challenged users to play against the clock, for the chance to win the phone.
Inside the stadium, Samsung Galaxy A5 campaign content featured in match day programs, court-side LED screens and on the Jumbo screen.
Snapchat integration
In an Australian first, a live Snapchat integration for the netball Grand Final on the Nine Network was developed.
